About Me

WILLIAM GEORGE C.S.C.S.

I've been a Personal Trainer for over Seven Years. In this time I've helped numerous clients reach an assortment of different goals. Whether your goal is to run a Marathon or enter a Powerlifting competition, I have the Experience to provide the Tools and Direction you need to get where you want to go.

Background

My first job in the Fitness industry was as a Front Desk Attendent at 24 Hour Fitness back in 2007. I quickly became a Personal Trainer in 2008 after I graduated from the University of Washington with double Bachelor's in Cell, Molecular and Developmental Biology and in Psychology. In this time I have been certfied by several national agenicies including AFAA, NASM, NCSF and NESTA.

Training Philosophy

Trends come and go like the wind in the Fitness industry and as a Trainer my responsibilty is to analyze these and see if I can use them to help clients reach their goals quicker. In my seven years the thing I've found is most constant is that all things can be achieved through strength. Whether your goal is to get long and lean or big and buff; a properly periodized strength and conditioning program will not only help you reach your goal, but also help you sustain it.
